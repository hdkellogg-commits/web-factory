- **Concept**: A high-fidelity, minimalist AI tool hub.

- **H1 (Transformational)**: "The Future of Professional AI, Curated for the Next Generation."

## 2. Color & Surface Styling
### 60-30-10 Distribution
- **60% Primary Surface**: `#F8F9FA` (Light) / `#0F172A` (Dark Mode ready).
- **30% Secondary (Brand)**: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue) – used for Navigation and Section Dividers.
- **10% Accent (Star)**: `#10B981` (Vibrant Green) for primary CTAs and success states.

### Surface Treatment
- **Grain Rule**: 2% opacity SVG noise filter applied to all backgrounds to prevent "flatness."
- **Glassmorphism**: 
  - `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`
  - `border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`
  - Applied to Navigation Bar and Floating Tool Cards.
- **Elevation**: Layered shadows (`0px 10px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`, `0px 2px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.02)`).

## 3. Motion Intent Handoff
### Entrance Sequence
1.  **Stage 1 (0ms)**: Hero Image fades in with a 1.05x subtle zoom.
2.  **Stage 2 (300ms)**: H1 character-by-character slide-up (Staggered).
3.  **Stage 3 (600ms)**: Primary CTA button fades in with "Haptic Pop" (subtle scale-up/down).

### Interaction Rhythm
- **Tool Cards**: 5px float up on hover with soft shadow expansion.
- **Navigation**: Magnetic hover effect on links (10px pull).

## 4. Divergence Heuristics (Variant B)
- **Grid Break**: Asymmetrical placement of the "Star" image (bleeding off the right edge).
- **Kinetic Type**: Scrolling marquee for "Latest AI Tool Integration" list in the footer.
- **Contrast**: High-vibrancy green buttons against deep navy backgrounds for maximum pop.
