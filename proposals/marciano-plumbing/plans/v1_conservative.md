# Strategic Structural Plan (v1_conservative) - Marciano Plumbing

**Goal:** Establish a high-performance, accessible Next.js 15 baseline focused on local lead generation.

## 1. Goal Hierarchy (@architect)
- **North Star Metric**: **High-Intent Lead Conversion** (24/7 Calls & Booking).
- **The Star of the Show**: **Emergency Availability & Mitsubishi Diamond Status**.

## 2. Semantic HTML5 Landmark Mapping
- `<header>`: Site ID, Utility (Call Now), 24/7 Badge.
- `<nav>`: Primary (Services, About, Reviews, Contact).
- `<main>`:
    - `<h1>`: Dedicated to "24/7 Emergency Plumber & HVAC Contractor in Woburn, MA."
    - `<section id="hero">`: Focus on high-contrast "Call Now" CTA + Mitsubishi Logo.
    - `<section id="trust">`: Content chunking for 4.8-star Hibu reviews.
    - `<section id="hvac">`: Specialized content for Ductless Mini-Splits/Boilers.
    - `<section id="emergency">`: Quick-response plumbing services.
- `<footer>`: Local Business Schema (NAP: Name, Address, Phone), Service Area Map.

## 3. Layout Rhythm & Spacing (12/8/4 System)
- **Hard Grid**: All margins/paddings strictly divisible by **8px**.
- **Container**: Max-width **1440px**; centered.
- **Rhythm**:
    - Intra-group (Headline/Sub): **16px** (2x baseline).
    - Inter-group (Content Blocks): **32px** (4x baseline).
    - Section Breathing: **96px** (12x baseline).

## 4. Performance & Core Web Vitals
- **Lighthouse Target**: 90/100 (Minimum).
- **Next/Image**: Priority loading for Hero/Mitsubishi assets.
- **Font**: System Serif/Sans-Serif stack for zero CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) in the conservative variant.
