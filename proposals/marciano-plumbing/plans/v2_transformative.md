# Transformative Design Plan (v2_bold) - Marciano Plumbing

**Goal:** Transform Marciano Plumbing into a premium, high-tech "Home Services Tech Leader" with a visual identity rooted in mechanical precision.

## 1. Visual Rhyme Audit (@innovator)
- **The Italic Slant**: All section dividers and card skew angles will use the **8-degree right-leaning slant** from the primary wordmark. This visually encodes "Speed" and "Industrial Motion."
- **Horizontal Separators**: 4px Charcoal (#1a1a1a) bars with negative space used to "chunk" complex HVAC service data.

## 2. Hero Section Concept (Primacy Effect)
- **Layout**: Asymmetrical Split (60/40).
- **Star of the Show**: **High-fidelity Mitsubishi Ductless Mini-Split** with technical HUD (Heads-Up Display) overlays showing efficiency ratings.
- **H1 Formula**: "Modern Efficiency meets 24/7 Reliability."
- **Motion Intent**: 
    - Entrance: Logo and Utility Call appear immediately.
    - Animation: Subtle slow-zoom center-screen on the Mitsubishi unit.
    - Kinetic: Pulsing 24/7 "Active" badge in the upper right.

## 3. Color & Surface Treatment (60-30-10)
- **60% Neutral (White/Silver)**: Clean, high-performance background.
- **30% Secondary (Charcoal #1a1a1a)**: Industrial "Toolbox" sections.
- **10% Accent (Tech Blue #0c82f8)**: High-contrast high-intent actions.
- **Surface**: 2% SVG "Metal Grain" filter applied to charcoal cards to prevent flat design fatigue.
- **Glassmorphism**: Emergency pop-outs and "Current Job Status" widgets use 10px backdrop blurs.

## 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **Headlines**: **Fjalla One** (Condensed Sans-Serif) for industrial impact.
- **Body**: **Schibsted Grotesk** (Clean UI Sans) for technical legibility.
- **Scale**: Major Third scaling to ensure dominance of the value proposition.
